Phantom Discord Bot

Phantom(v3.7) is a Discord Bot for development teams who use Discord as their communication form.

Need help using Phantom?

GitSoar + Phantom

GitSoar v7.1

Phantom allows developers to share their commits together through Discord, tracking multiple repositories on the same channel, track only one specific branch and more, thanks to GitSoar.

Phantom introduces the concept of tracking more than 1 repository at once. This allows tracking multiple tool repositories, make commits together and add people (GitHub Desktop) and everything you could imagine.

Phantom Instances

You can use Phantom in 2 forms:

  1. Use the Phantom Original Instance.

  2. Use a partner instance.

There's almost no difference between using the original or the partners instance. The connection is secure, and all our partners have the most recent Phantom version.

Take note that the original instance is online from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Getting started

1. Install Discord

Download and install Discord. It's free and cross-platform. You can use it on Windows, macOS, or Linux.

2. Invite Phantom to your server

Invite Phantom to your Discord server. If you need help, take a look at the Documentation page: "How do I configure Phantom?".
